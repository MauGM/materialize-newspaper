Tiger Escapes Bronx Zoo

Later found in a Dumpster

A tiger escaped The Bronx Zoo on Thursday at approximately 3:03PM. Although the tiger did not harm anyone, he was seen walking the streets of Brookyln calmly. By the time animal control found him, he was peacefully laying down in a dumpster playing with the neighborhood cats.

Ducks Abducted by Aliens

Found dead on the moon

Fellow aliens landed in the Central Park Pond and abducted all the ducks with their superdooper spaceship. NASA later reported that the same ducks were then found dead on the moon. New York City is mourning the loss of their ducks, and are hoping for an explanation.
